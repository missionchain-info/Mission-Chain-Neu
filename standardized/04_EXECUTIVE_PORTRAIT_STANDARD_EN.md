# Mission Chain - Executive Portrait Standard (Public Brand Version)

Version: v1.0
Date: 2026-02-21

## 1. Objective
Create a unified, professional, and trustworthy visual identity for all team members across white paper, website, press kits, and investor materials.

## 2. Required Visual Format
- Orientation: vertical portrait
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 (recommended) or 3:4 (fallback)
- Minimum export: 2400 x 3000 px
- File format: PNG (transparent or solid background) + JPG web variant
- Color profile: sRGB

## 3. Composition Standard
- Framing: from mid-torso to head with consistent headroom
- Pose: relaxed professional stance, neutral or light smile
- Eye line: direct or slightly off-camera, consistent per series
- Lighting: soft key + fill, no harsh shadows
- Sharpness: high face clarity and natural skin detail

## 4. Background Standard
Choose one global style and apply to all members:
- Option A (recommended): solid light neutral gray `#EDEDED`
- Option B: brand gradient background (single approved template)

Do not mix multiple unrelated background styles in one executive set.

## 5. Wardrobe Standard
- Executive/business attire aligned with role seniority
- Avoid mixed casual and formal looks within the same leadership tier
- No distracting logos or unapproved symbols

## 6. Text and Layout Rule
- Do not embed names/titles inside portrait image files
- Name/title must be rendered by the design layout layer (PDF/site), not baked into the image

## 7. Authenticity and Quality Control
- Avoid visual artifacts that reduce trust (distorted hands, watch/earring anomalies, over-smoothed skin)
- If AI-generated or AI-enhanced images are used, apply manual retouch QA before public release
- Maintain realistic proportions and identity consistency with official profiles

## 8. Filename Convention
`missionchain_{role}_{fullname}_{version}.png`

Examples:
- `missionchain_ceo_james_harstad_v1.png`
- `missionchain_cto_nirmal_mukherjee_v1.png`

## 9. Delivery Package
For each person, provide:
- Master PNG
- Web JPG
- Square crop (1:1 avatar)
- Thumbnail crop (for mobile cards)

## 10. QA Checklist (Per Image)
- correct aspect ratio
- no embedded text
- approved background
- approved wardrobe
- natural facial rendering
- no visible artifact
- file naming convention followed
