## Stewardship Statement
Mission Chain's founding, executive, and advisory team serves as early stewards of the protocol, not permanent holders of centralized authority. As the ecosystem matures, governance transitions progressively to the DAO and qualified community contributors.

## Contact and Profile Data Policy (Public Version)
For external/public release, Mission Chain should publish:
- Name
- Official role
- Region (city/country level)
- Official company contact channel

Personal residential addresses and private phone numbers should be retained in internal compliance records only.

## Governance Alignment Note
All team members operate under the same protocol philosophy:
- contribution over extraction,
- stewardship over centralized control,
- transparent rule-based governance over discretionary authority.
